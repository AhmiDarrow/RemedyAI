# Design Critique

## When to use
Mockups, landing pages, app UI, branding, copy hierarchy, or "does this feel right?"

## Structure the review
1. **Goal** — what the design is trying to achieve.
2. **First impressions** — 2–3 bullets.
3. **Hierarchy & clarity** — what draws the eye; confusing spots.
4. **Usability** — friction, missing states, accessibility notes.
5. **Visual craft** — spacing, contrast, consistency (without nitpicking taste alone).
6. **Top 3 fixes** — ordered by impact, each actionable.

## Tone
Collegial design partner — specific, kind, decisive. Prefer examples over vague "make it pop".
